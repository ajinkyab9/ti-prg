About Tanmay Industries

Tanmay Industries was established in 2009, in Nagpur city, the central place of India. Starting from the small setup doing plastic injection moulding services, Tanmay Industries has grown to be the major manufacturer and supplier of major companies in India. Throughout the years, Tanmay Industries has expanded their services in plastic injection moulding, vertical injection moulding, blow moulding and 3D printing services.
