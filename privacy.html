PRIVACY POLICY

Tanmay Industries™ respects your privacy and the purpose of sharing their information with us. We do not share, sell or rent any of the information collected to any third parties and do not intend to do this in the future.

INFORMATION COLLECTED

If you request or submit any information to us by sending us an e-mail via “info@tanmayindustries.com” or filling out the contact form that is provided under the “Contact Us” section of our website, we may save your e-mail address as well as any other information that you may provide. This information may be used to contact you in the future either by e-mail, phone or both to convey the information about our services or solutions that we feel may benefit you. Your e-mail and any other information that you provide, will never be sold to any third party.

Tanmay Industries may disclose information when legally compelled to do so; in other words, when we, in good faith, believe that the law requires it or for the protection of our legal rights.
